Competition Categories

Collection of categories used in Athletics competitions.

Default schema prefix: category:

IRI: http://purl.org/athletics/category#

Other categories may be defined creating instances of ath:CompetitiveAudience, giving a schema:name and schema:description, as well as the restrictions of gender schema:requiredGender and schema:requiredMinAge/schema:requiredMaxAge.
